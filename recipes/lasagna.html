Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian pasta dish made with layers of pasta sheets, rich meat sauce, creamy béchamel or ricotta, and melted cheese. It is hearty, comforting, and perfect for sharing.

This traditional oven-baked dish is ideal for family gatherings or special occasions. The layers blend together beautifully, creating a flavorful and satisfying meal.

Ingredientes

  • 12 lasagna sheets
  • 500 g ground beef
  • 2 cups tomato sauce
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 cups ricotta or béchamel sauce
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. Cook the lasagna sheets according to package instructions.
  3. Sauté onion and garlic, then add ground beef and cook until browned.
  4. Add tomato sauce, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 10 minutes.
  5. Spread a layer of meat sauce in a baking dish.
  6. Add a layer of lasagna sheets, ricotta or béchamel, and mozzarella.
  7. Repeat the layers and finish with mozzarella and Parmesan on top.
  8. Bake for 35–40 minutes until bubbly and golden.
  9. Let it rest for 10 minutes before serving.
