Peanut Soup

Description

Peanut Soup is a rich and creamy dish popular in West African cuisine. It combines peanuts, vegetables, and spices to create a warm and comforting meal full of flavor.

This soup can be made vegetarian or with chicken for extra protein. It has a smooth texture with a slightly spicy and nutty taste that makes it unique and delicious.

Ingredientes

  • 1 cup natural peanut butter
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon grated ginger
  • 2 cups diced tomatoes
  • 4 cups vegetable or chicken broth
  • 1 sweet potato, peeled and cubed
  • Salt and chili powder to taste

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a large pot over medium heat.
  2. Sauté onion, garlic, and ginger until soft.
  3. Add tomatoes and cook for 5 minutes.
  4. Stir in peanut butter and mix well.
  5. Add broth and sweet potato. Bring to a boil.
  6. Reduce heat and simmer for 20–25 minutes until sweet potato is tender.
  7. Season with salt and chili powder.
  8. Blend the soup if you prefer a smoother texture, then serve hot.
