Smash Potatoes

Description

Smash Potatoes are crispy on the outside and soft on the inside, making them a perfect side dish for almost any meal. They are first boiled until tender, then gently smashed and baked until golden and crunchy.

This recipe is simple, flavorful, and customizable. You can add garlic, herbs, cheese, or spices to match your taste. They are perfect for family dinners, parties, or as a delicious snack.

Ingredientes

  • 1 kg small potatoes
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • Salt to taste
  • Black pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (optional)

Steps

  1. Boil the potatoes in salted water until fork-tender (about 20 minutes).
  2. Preheat the oven to 220°C (425°F).
  3. Place the potatoes on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  4. Gently smash each potato using the bottom of a glass.
  5. Brush with olive oil, add garlic, salt, and pepper.
  6. Bake for 25–30 minutes until crispy and golden brown.
  7. Sprinkle with fresh parsley before serving.
